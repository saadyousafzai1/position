Education System in Pakistan:

Discover the Essence of Karachi

Structure: The education system in Pakistan is divided into several levels: Primary, Middle, Secondary, Higher Secondary, and Tertiary education. Primary education typically spans from grade 1 to grade 5, followed by middle school (grades 6 to 8) and secondary school (grades 9 to 10). Higher Secondary education comprises grades 11 and 12, leading to intermediate qualifications. Tertiary education includes undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by universities and other institutions
